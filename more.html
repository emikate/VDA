The first plot shows an interesting relationship between tracks that are instrumental and acoustic. The second plot shows an interesting relationship between genre and popularity. EDM appears around the 1980's with large shifts in popularity, becoming more normal after during the 2000s. Rock music also sky rockets and dips quite low during the 1960s-1970s, becoming more normal during the 1980s. The third plot shows a slighly lower impact on a tracks popularity if remix is true.
